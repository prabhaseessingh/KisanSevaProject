Soil Impact

Different soil types retain moisture differently. Clay and black soils can increase pest activity due to higher humidity, while sandy soils usually experience slightly lower pest pressure.

Irrigation Impact

Irrigation methods influence pest populations. Canal and borewell irrigation create humid crop environments that may increase pest activity, while drip irrigation reduces leaf moisture and chemical use.

Pest Pressure

Regions with warmer temperatures and higher humidity tend to have higher pest populations. Solar traps are more effective in such conditions because they continuously reduce pest breeding cycles.
